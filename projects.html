In To The Woods
(in early development)

In to the woods is a roguelite survival
game. You enter into the woods and
have to survive five ten minute days;
however, every time you enter, the woods
will shift and change.

Genesis Thrive
(currently in the concepting pass)

Genesis Thrive is a procedurally
generated open world focused on survival
and automation in an unfamiliar land.
Inspired by GregTech, Satisfactory, and Factorio.
Future plans include interplanetary travel,
trading with generated companies,
and building trade networks.
Procedural planets and machs
may be added as DLC.
